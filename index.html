Home of the Explorers

Kolby Samuels


La Salle University, the home of the Explorers.

In Philadelphia, The city of Brotherly Love.

A community of people, committed and dedicated.

They have no flaws but you be the judge.

Blue and Gold, the Explorers wear.

Where the Explorers are, victory is there.

With all of the fans out of their chairs.

Anyone who opposes should beware.

With all of La Salle chanting and roaring,

The Explorers forever, will be always exploring.
